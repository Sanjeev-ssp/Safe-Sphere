Energy efficiency is the cornerstone of our philosophy. Traditional systems tend to consume excessive power, leading to environmental strain and higher costs. Safe Sphere takes a low-energy, high-impact approach by integrating advanced techniques like voltage scaling, power gating, and adaptive clocking.
